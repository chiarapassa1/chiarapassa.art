Ancient materials, digital metamorphosis. Sculptures that exist between the weight of stone and the fluidity of code.

Neither sketch nor simulation

The digital 3D forms are not preliminary sketches or simulations. They are sculptural states in their own right, equal in weight to any physical form. A series of interactive 3D sculptures that reimagine ancient solid materials as fluid, mutable entities, revealing an alternative, visionary side of the mineral world.
