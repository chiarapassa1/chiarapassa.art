Augmented &
Virtual Realities

AR extends the sculptural surface. VR penetrates it to its point of origin.

A strange oscillation between spaces

In the Object Oriented Sculptures ecosystem, augmented reality extends the ceramic surface beyond its physical limits, animating, expanding, transforming it. Virtual reality goes further: it penetrates the form from within, bringing the viewer to the origin of the sculpture, where matter and code are still undivided.
